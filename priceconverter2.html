Fabric Price Conversion

Convert Fabric Price from KG to Yards

If you know fabric width and gsm

Formula:
Price per Yard= (Price per kg X Width of the fabric in meter X GSM)/1093.613


NB: Please note that in our calculetor you need to give width in cm, this will convert inch to meter in background

Convert Fabric Price from KG to Yards

If you know fabric width and gsm

Formula:
Price per Yard= (Price per kg X Width of the fabric in inch X 0.0254 X GSM)/1093.613


NB: Please note that in our calculetor you need to give width in inch, this will convert inch to meter in background

Formula:
Price per Yard/Meter= KG price/Converted Fabric (Yards/Meters)
