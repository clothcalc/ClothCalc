Consumption Calculator for Woven Garments

Woven Garments Consumption

Documentation for Woven Garments Consumption

Formula:

\[ \text{Fabric Consumption in CM(yards/pcs)} = \frac{\text{Body Length} \times \text{Body Width} \times \text{Fabric Part} \times \text{Unit Factor}}{\text{Fabric Width} \times \text{Unit Factor} \times \text{Unit Factor}} \]

\[ \text{Fabric Consumption in Inch(yards/pcs)} = \frac{\text{Body Length} \times \text{Body Width} \times \text{Fabric Part}}{\text{Fabric Width} \times \text{Unit Factor}} \]

Parameters:

  • Body Length: Length of the garment body in CM or Inch.
  • Body Width: Width of the garment body in CM or Inch.
  • Fabric Part: Fabric part of the garment.
  • Fabric Width: Width of the fabric in CM or Inch.
  • Unit Factor:
    • If the unit is CM: Unit factor \(91.4\) is use for convert cm to yard.
    • If the unit is Inch: Unit factor \(36\) is use for convert inch to yard.

Unit Selection:

User can choose CM or Inch for input values.

Usage:

  1. Input values for Body Length, Body Width, Fabric Part, and Fabric Width.
  2. Select the unit (CM or Inch).
  3. Click "Calculate Consumption."
  4. Result: Fabric Consumption in yards per piece.

Example in CM:

Body Length: 100 CM
Body Width: 50 CM
Fabric Part: 2
Fabric Width: 150 CM

\[ \text{Fabric Consumption in CM (yards/pcs)} = \frac{100 \times 50 \times 2 \times 91.4}{150 \times 91.4 \times 91.4} \]

Result: Fabric Consumption: 0.729 yards/pcs

Example in Inch:

Body Length: 40 Inch
Body Width: 20 Inch
Fabric Part: 2
Fabric Width: 60 Inch

\[ \text{Fabric Consumption in Inch (yards/pcs)} = \frac{40 \times 20 \times 2 }{60 \times 36 } \]

Result: Fabric Consumption: 0.741 yards/pcs
